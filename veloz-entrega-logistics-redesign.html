Protect Yourself from Fraud

At VELOZ ENTREGA LOGISTICS, the security of our customers is one of our highest priorities. Unfortunately, scammers sometimes impersonate logistics companies to steal money or personal information. Please stay alert and follow the guidance below.

We Will Never:

  • Ask you for permission to open your parcel once it is sealed after a shipment is created.
  • Request your online banking password, PIN, or one-time verification codes.
  • Ask you to pay unexpected fees through unofficial channels to release a shipment.
  • Contact you using unofficial email addresses or fake social media accounts while claiming to represent VELOZ.

Beware of Common Scams

  • Individuals requesting your tracking ID.
  • Phishing emails and text messages requesting your bank details.
  • Fraudulent job offers requesting application or training fees.
  • Fake customs, insurance, or delivery-fee requests.
  • Individuals claiming to be VELOZ employees using unofficial phone numbers or messaging apps.

How to Verify Communications

Confirm that any contact claiming to be from VELOZ comes from an official channel by reaching out to us directly before responding.

  • Always use the official VELOZ ENTREGA LOGISTICS website to track shipments.
  • If you receive a suspicious call, email, or message, contact our Customer Support before taking any action.

Official Telegram: t.me/VelozEL

Report Suspicious Activity

If you believe you have been contacted by someone impersonating VELOZ ENTREGA LOGISTICS, please report it immediately via our official Telegram: t.me/VelozEL

Stay Safe

Never share confidential personal or financial information with unverified individuals. If an offer, payment request, or message seems unusual or too good to be true, verify it with VELOZ ENTREGA LOGISTICS before responding.
